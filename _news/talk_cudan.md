Our lab director, Melvin Wevers, will present at the CUDAN Open Lab seminar series at Tallinn University. His talk, "The Architecture of Information: Uncovering Structure in Newspapers, Parliaments, and Administrative Archives," explores computational methods for analyzing historical and institutional information systems.

The seminar is part of CUDAN's Fall 2025 series, which brings together leading researchers in computational approaches to culture and society. The presentation will be followed by an extended discussion session, with the full seminar lasting approximately two hours.

The talk will be held in person at Tallinn University with simultaneous online access via Zoom. For Zoom details and more information about the seminar series, visit the [CUDAN events page](https://cudan.tlu.ee/events/). Previous CUDAN seminars are available on their [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@CUDANLab).
